Types of Services.

1.Portrait Photography

Portrait Photography or portraiture is a type of Photographyaimed towards capturing the personality of a person or group of poeople by using effective lighting, backdrops, different poses etc. Location, lighting, Composition, emotion, and technical settting.When all of five of these elements are well executed a great Portrait is created. There are many types of portrait photography, including traditional, environmental, candid, self, couple, group, constructionist, glamour, and fine art.

2.Event Photography

Event photography is the art of capturing high-quality images at a variety of events, from weddings to corporate gatherings to music festivals. The goal is to create a visual narrative of the event that encapsulates its spirit and atmosphere. Event photographers use a combination of technical skill, creativity, and an ability to connect with people to capture the emotions, interactions, and details that make each event unique.Here are some tips for event photography:

  • Understand the event
  • Have backup equipment
  • Use appropriate equipment
  • Back up original files

3.Videography

Videography is the art of capturing moving images electronically, typically using a digital camera, and then editing and post-producing them. Videographers often work on smaller projects, such as weddings, live events, or concerts, and are usually a one-person team or small crew.Photography is used in many fields, including:-

  • Art
  • Business
  • Journalism
  • Occasional events
  • Etc.

4.Drone Shoots

Drone photography and video is the use of a remote-controlled drone to capture still images and videos from the air. Drones can be used in many industries, including film, journalism, and security. Some drone features includes:

  • Smart mode
  • Follow-me
  • Geofence
  • Etc.
